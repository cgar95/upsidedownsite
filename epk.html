upsidedow.n.smileyface


“Just cuz' I can do it like this, don't mean that I can't do it like that.” Begininng his debut ep with an energetic verse in a vibrant tenor, upsidedow.n.smileyface then rips into a rapid-fire rap that details his creative process: breaking the rules and making it up as he goes along.



upsidedow.n.smileyface grew up singing along to the local oldies radio station, and taught himself guitar so he could write music of his own. Influenced by avant-garde jazz, delta blues, hard rock, and everything in between, he captures the range of human emotion with genre-bending compositions that feature his witty lyricism and dynamic voice.

Taking on the moniker of the playfully polysemous emoji after the COVID-19 pandemic broke up his previous band and canceled all their remaining gigs, upsideodw.n.smileyface wouldn't let anything stop him from making music. He booked a session at the local recording studio, assembled the best musicians he could find, and started writing material for his debut solo release.

After months of writing, rehearsing, and recording, he had created Funky Magic: a 5 song ep showcasing upsidedow.n.smileyface's ability to fuse his unfettered energy with soulful contemplation through a mix of jazz, rock, hip-hop, and reggae.
